How it works

From job search to submitted application in three steps.

search

1. Search & Source

The agent crawls company career pages and LinkedIn to find roles that match your specific profile and skill variants.

auto_awesome

2. Tailor & Generate

Claude analyzes the job description to generate a role-specific CV and cover letter, emphasizing your most relevant experience.

check_circle

3. Review & Submit

The agent fills the ATS form automatically. You do a final review of the EEO fields and click submit. Claude never submits without you.
